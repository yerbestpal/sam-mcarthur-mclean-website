Game Design Portfolio

Current Position: Game Designer at Aurorian Studios working on One Lonely Outpost

My name is Samuel Mcarthur-Mclean, I am a game designer from Glasgow and have just been awarded a First Class Honours degree in Computer Games Design from Glasgow Caledonian University. I am looking for a job as a game designer at an ambitious studio.

In 2019 I was able to attend an event called Resonate to show off one of my games, Cyber Sphere allowing the public to play it. This experience was valuable in helping me grow as a designer.

Another game I worked on called Novoslavia was downloaded by a youtuber who did a let’s play, with it currently having over 370,000 views. The response from viewers was very positive towards the game and even led to a few hundred downloads of the it.

My influences come from a wide variety of genres, from cyberpunk to Korean cinema to Scottish novels, all coming together to make something interesting.

From 2014 – 2017 I worked as a journalist where I wrote news, reviews and previews from events such as EGX. Going to events like these and meeting developers made me realize I wanted to make games and not just write about them.
